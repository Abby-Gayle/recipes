Chicken Flautas

Description

This is chicken flautas. If you ever wanted an easy and delicous recipe this is it. Its just mainly tortilla with some chicken. See below how to create this very simple dish.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thighs (4-6 Pieces)
  • Corn Tortillas
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Onion
  • Vegtable Oil
  • Tooth Picks

Steps

  1. Prep Chicken: We will cook the chicken in some water. Once boiled add a piece of onion and a pinch of salt to the chicken. It should cook for about 45 min.
  2. Shred Chicken: Take the chicken out once cool to the hands. We will then shred the chicken then add a little more salt and a little pepper.
  3. Add to Heated Tortillas: Heat the tortillas up then add the chicken to them. Roll them up and stick half a toothpick through them so they dont open.
  4. Time to Fry: Add oil to a pan and put your flautas in the oil and make them as crispy as you want
