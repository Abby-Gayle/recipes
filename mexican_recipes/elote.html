Elote

Description

This is an elote which is grilled corn smothered in creamy mayo sauce and topped with chilli powder, crumbled cheese, and lime. To create this awesome snack just gather the ingredients and follow the steps below!

Ingredients

  • Cobs of Corn
  • Butter
  • Mayo
  • Crema Mexicana
  • Cotija Cheese
  • Chilli Powder
  • lime wedge

Now that we have the ingredents lets go ahead and follow the steps to create the recipe!

Steps

  1. Cook the Corn: Cook the cobs on the stove get a large pot of salted water to boil over medium-high heat. Add the ears of corn and cook until thender, about 10 minutes.
  2. Spread with Butter: Spread melted butter over the cooked corn and grill until slightly charred on all sides.
  3. Add Crema Mixture: Mixx the mayonnaise and Mexican crema together, then spread a thin layer over the cooked corn cobs.
  4. Enjoy: Sprinkle generously with cotija cheese and then desired amount of chilli powder. Serve garnished with cilantro and a lime wdge on the side, for squeezing on top. Enjoy and eat the Elote!
