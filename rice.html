Mexican Rice

Description

This dish is a tasty side dish. Mexican rice can be added to any dish as a side item. See below how to create this side dish.

Ingredients

  • Long-grain white rice
  • Chicken Broth
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Chilli Powder
  • Ground Cumin
  • Garlic Salt
  • Vegetable Oil

Steps

  1. Cook the Rice: In a large pot cook the rice in the oil for 3 to 4 minutes, until translucent.
  2. Stir all Together: Stir in the rest of ingredients.
  3. Boil: Bring mixture to a boil.
  4. Relax and Wait: Cover and simmer for 25 minutes.
